FTC Seasons!

SkyStone
2019-2020

This seasons tasks compiled of stacking bricks on to the foundation and repeatedly moving it to and from the building site while staying under a strict height constraint.We had to stack loose- fitting, unstable bricks up as high as we could go on a moveable foundation. The team would get extra points if they moved the foundation in and out of the building zone throughout the different parts of the competition. There were two different height skybridges that we would have to fit under. The upper skybridge is 20 inches and the lower skybridges are 14 inches. Going under the upper skybridge will earn the team no points, going under your teams color lower skybridge will earn you 2 points and going under the opposite colors lower skybridge will be a deduction of 2 points.
